How It Works

Open the proposal and go through every option. Each card has pricing, photos, amenities, and my personal notes on why I picked it for you.

2

Save your favorites

Tap the heart on anything you like. Your picks will show up in the "My Picks" panel at the top so you can see your running total.

Submit your picks

Once you're happy with your selections, hit "Send My Picks to Nathan" at the bottom of the picks panel. I'll get notified right away.

4

I'll handle the rest

I'll send you a final quote with everything confirmed. You enter your payment info, and I'll get the whole trip booked.
