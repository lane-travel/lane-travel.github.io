The Full Process

From intake form to packed bags

Here's exactly what happens after you pay your booking fee — step by step, no surprises.

1
You
Fill out the trip intake form
After you pay, you'll get a link to a quick form. Tell me your destination, dates, budget, vibe, and what matters most. This is what I build your entire trip around — so the more detail, the better.
2
Nate
I send you a custom proposal
I research hotels, experiences, dining, and anything that fits your trip. You'll get a personalized proposal page with multiple options to browse — hotels with photos, pricing, descriptions, and my honest take on each one. Not a generic list — real options curated for you.
3
You
Pick the options you love
Go through the proposal on your own time. Select which hotel, which experiences, and which add-ons you want. You can mix and match. No calls required — though I'm always available if you have questions.
4
Nate
I create a final quote and collect payment info
Based on what you picked, I put together a final quote with the total cost. Once you approve it, I'll collect your card info through a secure link to start booking everything.
5
Nate
I book your entire trip
Hotels, experiences, dinner reservations, show tickets — whatever you selected, I book it all. I never do flights (that's on you), but everything else is handled.
6
Nate
Confirmations come in as I book
As each booking is confirmed, I forward the confirmation your way. You'll see them trickle in — hotels, reservations, tickets — so you always know where things stand.
7
Nate
You get a final trip packet + interactive itinerary
Once everything is booked, I send you one clean email with every confirmation organized in one place — plus a full interactive digital itinerary. Your itinerary includes your full day-by-day schedule, hotel details, check-in/check-out times, reservation info, directions, tips, maps, and everything you need in one shareable page. It's your entire trip in your pocket.
8
Nate
Day before your trip: final itinerary resend + last call
The day before you leave, I resend your digital itinerary so it's right at the top of your inbox. This is also your last chance to add the SMS Concierge if you haven't already — because once your trip starts, I can't help you on the fly without it.
